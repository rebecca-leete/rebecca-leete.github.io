Being Mindful of my Body

I have dabbled in meditation and mindfulness before, but I have never heard about "high resolution perception" or the function of the amygdala. As someone who experiences anxiety from time to time the idea of increasing my awareness of the physical sensations connected to my emotions and being able to manage how I feel based on my body is really intriguing to me. I e nded up doing 20 minutes of mindfulness, but it was right before bed and I was pretty tired, so I found myself very distracted and not present, but I' m eager and motivated to continue this process and see how I develop from it.
