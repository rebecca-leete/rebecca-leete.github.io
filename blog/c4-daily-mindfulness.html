Making Mindfulness Millenial-Friendly

Checking In on Mindfulness Practices

So I actually already do a daily practice of answering a series of questions I have set myself for personal reflection and habit building. It's amazing! You should all do it! And actually the first of my questions is "Did you spend 15 minutes of peaceful silent time with yourself?" Doing this reflective practice with a other person is doubly effective - there is something about that external accountability that I personally find more motivating.

Having someone you can check in with weekly or daily is beneficial for any habit-building, as it gives you the space to be able to reflect on what you feel like you're doing well and not doing well, and whether it's helpful and purposeful or not.
