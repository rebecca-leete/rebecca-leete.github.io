Technical Blog I

Time and Habits

Looking at how to build more effective time management skills and work habits. Not as boring as it sounds, turns out our brain is a fascinating creature (who knew) and understanding how it works goes a huge way to increasing your productivity and creativity!

Technical Blog II

HTML, CSS and the DOM

Here we're looking at the three basic structural foundations of a website and beginning to explore the process of putting a structured webpage together. Turns out there are designs within designs within designs! As strange as it's been to get my head around it, it's actually made things a whole lot simpler!

Technical Blog III

Layout and Responsiveness

Wandering further into webpage structure, we start to get a deeper understanding of how a page's elements fit together, to the extent that we are understanding and guiding what happens when you look at a webpage on different sized-screens. This is especially important when we think about how people access the internet now from computers, but also phones, tablets, etc.

Technical Blog IV

Design to Web

Here we have a look at some of the elements involved in moving a website from an idea in our head to an actual functioning entity on the internet. Having just rebuilt what you're looking at from scratch, I can tell you it's quite an involved process, but incredibly frustrating, fascinating, and, finally, fulfilling.

Javascript 101

Leaving the realms of HTML and CSS for bigger and better things, JavaScript has reminded me just how un-like humans and computers think. I say this because I get people. I get how they think. But JavaScript is in every way a completely different language. Have a look at some basic js concepts I struggle to explain.
